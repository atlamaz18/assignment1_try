History/Accomplishment's of Erbakır Science High School

  • Erbakır Science High School gave its first graduates in 1999 after it started education.
  • While our school prepared our 45 students for life in 1999, achieved 100% success as a placement rate.
  • 951 students graduated from 1999 to 2012, 936 of that students enabled to be settled and created a successful graphic with 95% placement.
  • Our school in 2011, showing a 98% success in science high schools of its kind in Turkey has been the 1st.
  • In 2011 YGS LYS exam, according to the average net, Erbakır became the 1st in chemistry and 3rd in math, physic and biology in Turkey.
  • In 2012 TUBITAK Scientific Olympics, Erbakır becomes runner-up. And also in 2013, 2 of our projects won the regional qualifications in the TUBITAK Scientific Project competition.
  • In 2013, our students won the 2nd prize in the Acıbadem University project competition.
  • The students of our school, which won the Chess Provincial Championship in 2013, came in 2nd in the international tournament.
